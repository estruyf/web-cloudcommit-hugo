How to start
creating your
own event.

Most of us are stuck at home, and with all conferences canceled or moved online. There is now way to escape home any time soon.

As it is April 1st, why not create your fake event to tell your colleagues/partner you cannot miss this event. Just create the following URL: https://cloudcommit.io/:year/:month/:day/:location/:event-name/

Here is an example: https://cloudcommit.io/2020/10/25/Belgium-Antwerp/The-greatest-event/
